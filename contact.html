Contact Us

It is better to have some backup plan if we things go sideways, first we land on Nepal it is recommended to take their SIM/mobile phone services which will be provided in their Airport or regular phone shops, in mountains there will be selected regions where sim services are available. Second, it is better have a list of emergency contact numbers for us and for fellow travellers and for all people, who are in need.
